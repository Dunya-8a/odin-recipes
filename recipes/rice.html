Persian Rice

Traditional Persian rice with safran

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large potato, cut into 0.7cm slices
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • olive oil
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon parsley, or to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice to get the starch out.
  2. While doing that, bring the water to the boil, adding a pinch of salt.
  3. Add the rice and cook for 7 minutes.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Cover bottom of pot with 1 layer of potato slices. Sprinkle cumin and salt over potatoes. Cook until potatoes are sizzling, 2 to 3 minutes; top potatoes with rice to form an even layer. Reduce heat to low and place butter slices over rice.
  5. Top pot with a layer of clean paper towels and place lid over towels. Steam until rice is fluffy, about 45 minutes.
  6. Grind saffron threads with a mortar and pestle. Mix crushed saffron with 1 1/2 tablespoons hot water in a large bowl. Add a couple spoonfuls of rice to saffron mixture and stir until rice is yellow.
  7. Spoon remaining rice into a serving bowl, top with saffron rice, and line edges of bowl with potatoes. Garnish with parsley.
  8. Enjoy!
