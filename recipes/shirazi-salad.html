Iranian/Persian Shirazi Salad

A wonderful vegetarian side dish to a heavy meal!

The special ingredient in this dish a the Middle Eastern spice sumac (my personal favourite!). You should be able to get it from most Middle Eastern stores.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 2 small cucumbers
  • 1 tbsp of dried mint, dried dill and ground sumac
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Cube the tomatoes, onion and cucumbers (preferably small pieces but do as you like).
  2. Mix in a bowl with all spices, lemon juice and olive oil. Salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!
